Mustard Chicken

Description

This is great for those who are tired of eating grilled chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 Onion and 1 green
  • Mustard
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Seal the chicken and set away
  2. Cut the onions
  3. Reheat the onions
  4. Add the cream and mustard
  5. Add the breast
  6. Finish with green onion
  7. You did it!
