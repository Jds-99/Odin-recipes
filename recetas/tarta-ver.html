Veggie Pie

Description

It is an exquisite recipe, very light and easy to make. Very good vegan option

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • Pie dough
  • 1 onion
  • 2 eggplant
  • 2 zucchini
  • cream

Steps

  1. Cut onions, eggplant and zucchini in small pieces
  2. Grate the carrots
  3. Reheat all vegetables and season it
  4. Add the cream
  5. Assemble the cake with the dough in an oven dish
  6. Bake it for 20 minutes
  7. You did it!
